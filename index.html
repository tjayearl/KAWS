About KAWS

Brian Donnelly, known professionally as KAWS, is an American artist and designer. His work includes repeated use of a cast of figurative characters and motifs, some dating back to the beginning of his career in the 1990s. Initially a graffiti artist, KAWS subverted imagery on billboards, bus shelters, and phone booth advertisements, which brought him notoriety.
